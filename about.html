About Us
The national University of Samoa is the top in the whole of Samoa

History

The National University of Samoa is the only national university in Samoa. Established in 1984 by an act of parliament, it is coeducational and provides certificate, diploma, and undergraduate degree programs, as well as technical and vocational training.
‍

Vision

A vibrant and Innovative University of Excellence in Research, Samoan Studies, Quality Education and Training which is responsive to National Development Needs.
‍

Mission

Our Mission is to produce quality graduates from across all disciplines to meet the nation’s human resource needs.
